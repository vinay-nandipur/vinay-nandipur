My Thoughts on Improving Interview Process(Talent Aquisition):

  • Talent selection should not be based on memory power(Example: There is many technical exams like OSCP certification allows users to take tests by referring online).
  • It's okay to take a break in between professional career, I don't understand why taking a break in professional career is a problem for some people/organisations.
  • It's good if there is a snadbox access given & assign a demo project to work on, so that, capable person can showcase their talent.
  • It's always not run against time, what I mean is, asking a person to develop a app/write a automation script in 10 minutes and deciding on that is really not fair.
  • In fact, there is no need for a panel or a particular person to take technical interview, complete process can be automated.
  • People attending the automated technical interview can enable the webcam & live record themselves while taking automated interviews by explaining what ever the steps they are going through.
  • Automated technical interviews are useful just because panel member/s (not all but some of them) try to de-motivate the person attending the interview, which can be avoided easily with automated technical interviews.
